OpenDiner —

An app to designed to alleviate the anxiety and awkwardness of dining alone while traveling.

Date — Winter 2016
Class — Mobile Design
Instructor — David Conrad

When I was abroad in summer of 2015, I spent a couple of days by myself in Rome, Italy—land of pizza, pasta, and gelato. Yet somehow, I found myself frequenting McDonald's by myself. Luckily, I had company soon with whom I enjoyed many traditional Italian three-course dinners with, but I still can't imagine doing so by myself.

OpenDiner is an app concept that I designed as one way to begin addressing this experience. Via OpenDiner, you can make "open reservations"—reservations at restaurants that other people can join—or join another open reservation. OpenDiner mimics how you might get introduced to someone in real life—you are at the same place, at the same time.
