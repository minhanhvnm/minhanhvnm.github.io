About Me —

Hi, I’m Minh-Anh. I’m designer from Seattle, WA. I'm interested in using design to as a communication tool to help people better understand complex topics.

I'm currently an interactive designer at Gartner for Marketers (formerly L2), where I design interactive web pieces to communicate business intelligence research.

I graduated from the University of Washington's Visual Communication Design in 2017. My past experience includes interning at Rice Creative, a design studio in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

When not designing, you can find me taking dance classes in NYC, painstakingly curating my Spotify playlists, taking photos, or eating spicy food.
