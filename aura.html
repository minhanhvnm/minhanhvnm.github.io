Mixed Reality

Aura

A mixed-reality museum experience that allows museum visitors to experience the stories of artifacts in an immersive space.

Date — Winter 2017
Duration — Jan 2017 to Mar 2017

Aura is a Mixed Reality museum experience that utilizes mixed reality technology to provide contextual information to museum artifacts. Museums today—and especially science and natural history museums—already are building immersive spaces: from the Pacific Science Center’s butterfly house, to planetariums, to details such as “environmental” murals in museum spaces. We felt that mixed reality technology could push museum engagement further by providing immersive experiences that augments a user’s understanding of that object. Aura does this by putting users in mixed reality environments that are attached to real, physical artifacts. These mixed reality environments includes animated versions of artifacts, environments brought to life with scale and sound, and integration of interactive, collaborative components.
