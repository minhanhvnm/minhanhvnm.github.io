The Set Designer Who Saw The Future —

A short informational video on Norman Bel Geddes' design work and philosophy.

Date — Winter 2017
Duration — 1 Week

Norman Bel Geddes: The Set Designer Who Saw The Future from Minh-Anh Nguyen on Vimeo.

Making informational videos is something I have wanted to do for a long time; I found Norman Bel Geddes an interesting figure in Design History, whose work had huge implications that was really influential in the development of the modern American highway system.

This project was my first spending extensive time in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, and writing and producing a video of this length by myself. I also wrote the script and sketched storyboards prior to producing the video.

If I had to go back and change anything, I would adjust the narrative to make the video more cohesive, and more deeply consider what portions of the narrative were important to underscore with visuals.
